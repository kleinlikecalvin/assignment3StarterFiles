I'm also a proud cat mom! I adopted Joonior in October of 2020 when she was just 6 weeks old!

This site isn't about me though, it's about Joon. Joon asked me to develop this site so she can share all the great times and adventures she's been having since her adoption. In the first six weeks of her life she was born, obviously, then removed from the care of her mother because she couldn't provide a healthy upbringing. Some giant scooped her and her siblings up, put them in a car, and drove them to a big facility where there are all sorts of other animals. Once there, Joon was examined then placed in a cage with her siblings. The cage was in a room with a lot of other cats. People would come and see her but never took her. When I met her, she was scared and not interested in giants. She wanted to be back in the cage with her brother where she felt safe. I gave her some time to adjust and started to notice little blips of her bold personality shine through. The moment I knew this was my new baby was when I scritchy scratched her little baby butt and she loved it. I knew then that she had a great personality and we'd get along just fine once she was in a healthy trusting environment. Before she could come home with me, she had to get a hysterectomy. I'm sure this was a traumatic experience for her, but it was comforting to me. I used to be the only youngin' I knew who had a hysterctomy, but I was no longer alone and neither was she.
